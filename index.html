blooddonation

A webappp that helps people donate and receive blood when required using an online database. The app has the following options.

1.A user can volunteer to donate blood

2.A user can regiser itself as a blood donating volunteer by providing details like its blood group etc.

3.A user can ask for blood in the time of emergency.The users detail will be sent out to all registered users with the contact details of the person in need.

The app sends out emails to all registered user whenever someone is in a need of blood,with details like blood group required,location etc.
